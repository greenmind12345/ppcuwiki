Peppa Pig The Movie


The first film in the PPCU, and first in the Minerals Saga.


Summary:


After half of all life in the universe is snapped away by Thanos, Peppa Pig assembles a team of heroes (Normal Face, Steve, Marvin) to fight back.



Notable characters:


*Peppa Pig


*Normal Face


*Steve


*Marvin


*The Hamburglar


*Thanos


*Agent Blueberries (Unnamed; post-ending scene)



Trivia:


Originally, this was all there was. SuperNova28 has no intentions to make a sequel, let alone a whole cinematic universe. The cinematic universe idea was thought up during a school lunch where one of Nova's friends brought up the idea.


Thanos doesn't use a single Infinity Stone power. He only uses his-as SuperNova28 calls it-"iconic giant purple f***ing fist".


Everyone resurrected by Marvin was meant to join the Avengers, but SuperNova28 had other ideas for members and didn't want the roster getting too big.


Hamburglar's betrayal was called "top 3 anime betrayals" by one of SuperNova28's IRL friends.
