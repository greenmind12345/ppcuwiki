Heisenberg


The fifth film in the PPCU, and fifth in the Minerals Saga.


Summary:


In an attempt to get the DEA off their tail, Walter White and Jesse Pinkman infuse their meth with the powers of the Mind Stone; inadvertantly infecting DEA Agent Hank Schrader, and sending him on a mad quest for more power.
