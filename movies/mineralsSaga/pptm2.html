Peppa Pig The Movie 2: The Multiverse Of Madness


The second film in the PPCU, and second in the Minerals Saga.


Summary:


After the fight with Thanos, the Avengers inadvertently created a rip in the multiverse. The rip caught the attention of the Multiverse Protection Agency, the governing body of the whole multiverse, and its sworn protectors; MPA Agent Blueberries was sent to assist the Avengers in repairing the rip.

Trivia:


Originally, the villain was named Peppa.AVI, who would've used her "epik demon powah!" to tear the multiverse apart. Was scrapped because it was a dumb idea; Nova thought it would make it too unserious.


SuperNova28 considers this one to be her favorite due to "the mixture of the goofy charm the first 3 had, and seriousness".


The MPA wouldn't have existed if it wasn't for this movie, as the entire reason the PPCU has a focus on the multiverse is because Nova thought it would be funny to reference "Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness".
